We’re married!

on 14 May 2016, Mobile, AL
Katie Isbell
David Zemon

Bride’s story

Born in Mobile, AL to Debbie and Roy Isbell, Katie has always had a passion for technology, fitness and animals. Her musical career began with piano at age six and flourished through college, where she was first chair trumpet freshman year at Samford University. Sophomore year brought her to Missouri S&T, where she graduated magna cum laude with a B.S. in both Computer Science and Computer Engineering. She now works at Union Pacific Railroad as an Application Developer.

Groom’s story

David was born in St. Louis, MO to Bonnie Whitfield and Art Zemon. At a young age, he swam for the SCCY Stingrays under Mary O'Mara. He participated on the team year-round for nine years, at which point the team was like a second family to him. He is also a car enthusiast, specifically Volvo, Saab and Koenigsegg. In his spare time he enjoys developing low-level software. He earned his B.S in Computer Engineering at Missouri S&T and now works at Union Pacific Railroad.

We met one cold, winter day in Dr. Stressful's class. It was boring, Katie was struggling, and David came to the rescue. With David's radiant smile and trusty laptop, we were able to escape Circuits 1 with GPAs unscathed. Our bond grew with time, and in the Spring of 2014, David gave Katie a shiny rock and Katie gave David a "Yes!"
