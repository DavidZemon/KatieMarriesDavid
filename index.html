Bride’s story

Born in Mobile, AL to Debbie and Roy Isbell, Katie has always had a passion for technology, fitness and animals. Her musical career began with piano in the second grade and flourished through college, where she was first chair trumpet freshman year at Samford University. Sophomore year brought her to Missouri S&T, where she graduated magna cum laude with a B.S. in both Computer Science and Computer Engineering.

Groom’s story

David was born in St. Louis, MO to Bonnie Whitfield and Art Zemon. In third grade, he swam for the SCCY Stingrays under Mary O'Mara. He participated on the team year-round for nine years, at which point the team was like a second family to him. He is also a car enthusiast, specifically Volvo, Saab and Koenigsegg. In his spare time he loves developing low-level software. He attended Missouri S&T and earned his B.S. in Computer Engineering.

We met one cold, winter day in Dr. Stressful's class. It was boring, Katie was failing and David came to the rescue. With his radiant smile and trusty laptop, we were able to escape Circuits 1 with GPAs unscathed. Our bond grew with time and, in the Spring of 2014, David gave Katie a shiny rock and Katie gave David a "Yes!"
